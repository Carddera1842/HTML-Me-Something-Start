Fun Facts For Our Fuzzy Friends

Brown Bears Have Cute Little Ears To Let You Know That They Mean No Harm!

If you look closely, Brown Bears have round, fluffy ears that they will let you pet if you just ask. Dont worry, they never turn down pets!

Zoom in to see the cuteness!

Brown Bears Don't Eat Meat...All The Time!

Being mostly vegetarian, Brown Bears sometimes eat nasty fish from a stream so you don't have to!

How Nice of Them!

Brown Bears Hate Bear Spray!

Some people may say that its the best bear deterant, but why wouldn't you want a nice bear hug? Brown Bears love to make new connections!

Aww, life long friends!
Brown Bears Love Sharing!

Realized you packed too much food for your trip? Just invite your favorite Brown Bear over! Some awful people may tell you it's "safer" to tie it up in a tree. They just hate sharing...and Brown Bears!

Which one of these people look happier?
Where to Meet Your Own Brown Bear Pal!

Brown Bears live all over the world. They like to live near humans because they are so friendly. Sometimes when they are especially lonely, Brown Bears will leave their numbers on park benches and stalls so that humans can call them directly!

For a good time, call Brown Bear!
As to can see, Brown Bears are Man's real best friend. Many people just want to give Brown Bears a bad name so that they can get more attention.
